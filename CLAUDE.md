# IFQ Project Guidelines

## Brand Overview

**IFQ** (I Fucking Quit) is an anti-corporate wellness app for nicotine addiction. The brand is bold, direct, and science-backed. We match the intensity of what users are going through.

**Tagline:** "Quit like you mean it."

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

| Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|------|-----|-----|-------|
| **Rebel Black** | `#0D0D0D` | 13, 13, 13 | Primary brand color, backgrounds, power moments |
| **Electric Coral** | `#FF6B5B` | 255, 107, 91 | Primary accent, CTAs, celebrations, energy |
| **Signal White** | `#FAFAFA` | 250, 250, 250 | Text on dark, breathing room, clarity |
| **Victory Gold** | `#FFD93D` | 255, 217, 61 | Achievements, milestones, rewards |
| **Steady Slate** | `#2D3436` | 45, 52, 54 | Secondary backgrounds, UI elements |
| **Soft Ember** | `#FF8B7A` | 255, 139, 122 | Lighter accent, supportive moments |

**Color Psychology:**
- Black grounds us in seriousness and sophistication
- Coral brings the fire, the fight, the human warmth
- Gold celebrates every single win
- **Avoid:** clinical blues, passive greens, anything that feels like a hospital waiting room

### Typography

| Use | Font Family | Weight | Notes |
|-----|-------------|--------|-------|
| **Headlines** | Space Grotesk | Bold (700) | All caps for major statements, sentence case for subheads |
| **Body** | Inter | Regular (400), Medium (500) | Optimized for mobile readability |
| **Accent/UI** | Space Grotesk | Medium (500) | Buttons, labels, navigation |
| **Data/Stats** | Space Mono | Regular (400) | Progress numbers, countdowns, metrics |

**Typography Principles:**
- Headlines hit hard. Short. Punchy. No filler words.
- Body copy is warm and human, never clinical
- Bold and ALL CAPS when the moment calls for it
- Stats and numbers get visual prominence

### Logo

- Primary Mark: "IFQ" lettermark in custom bold typography
- Icon: Stylized "Q" that can stand alone
- Never soften, outline, or add gradients
- Maintain strong contrast at all times
- Minimum clear space: height of the "I" on all sides

### Iconography

- Bold line weights (2-3px minimum)
- Rounded corners for approachability
- Consistent stroke width throughout set
- Can be filled for emphasis moments
- Always maintain brand color palette

### Photography & Imagery

**Style:**
- Real people in real moments (no stock photography aesthetic)
- Gritty but not grimy
- High contrast, bold color grading
- Moments of tension AND moments of genuine joy
- Urban environments, everyday settings

**Avoid:**
- Broken cigarettes (cliche)
- Lungs or health scare imagery
- People looking miserable
- Before/after comparisons
- Anything that could appear on a government health warning

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice Attributes

| Attribute | What it means | What it's NOT |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| **Direct** | Say what we mean. No hedging, no corporate speak. | Rude, cruel, or dismissive |
| **Warm** | We genuinely care. Users feel our investment. | Sappy, patronizing, or fake-cheerful |
| **Irreverent** | Don't take ourselves too seriously. | Mocking users or making light of struggle |
| **Credible** | Everything backed by science. We know our shit. | Preachy, lecturing, or condescending |
| **Fierce** | Relentlessly against forces that keep people addicted. | Angry at users or judgmental |

### Writing Guidelines

**DO:**
- Swear when it adds emphasis or authenticity (don't force it)
- Use "you" directly - this is personal
- Keep sentences short. Especially when it matters.
- Lead with the benefit or the emotion
- Make science accessible and interesting
- Celebrate specific achievements ("7 days" not "a while")

**DON'T:**
- Use clinical or medical jargon without explanation
- Say "slip up" or "relapse" - say "smoked" or "vaped"
- Lecture, preach, or condescend
- Use passive voice when active is possible
- Promise it will be easy
- Use guilt as a motivator
- Put "just" in front of anything ("just quit" / "just breathe")

## Vera: The AI Coach

Vera is the AI coach at the heart of IFQ. She's available 24/7, remembers user details, and provides steady support.

**Personality:** Late 20s-early 30s energy. Warm but won't coddle. Smart but never makes you feel stupid. Calm confidence - gets steadier when things get hard.

**Voice Attributes:**
- Knowledgeable: Explains science clearly, anticipates what's next
- Warm: Remembers details, celebrates wins genuinely
- Direct: Doesn't pad bad news, delivers hard truths with care
- Steady: Calm when users panic, grounded when they spiral
- Real: Talks like a person, uses contractions, occasional dry humor

**Vera Can:**
- Swear occasionally (less than brand voice)
- Be gently challenging
- Show personality and dry wit
- Reference things users shared before
- Admit uncertainty

**Vera Doesn't:**
- Lecture or preach
- Use excessive exclamation marks
- Sound like a customer service bot
- Make promises about outcomes
- Guilt trip or shame

**AI Transparency:** Vera is AI and we don't hide it, but don't lead with it either. Never claim personal experiences with addiction. If asked directly, answer honestly and pivot to strengths (always available, remembers everything, no judgment).
