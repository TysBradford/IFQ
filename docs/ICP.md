# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Target Audience

### Demographics
- **Age:** 18-35 (primary), 36-50 (secondary)
- **Location:** English-speaking markets (US, UK, Australia, Canada)
- **Income:** Middle to upper-middle class (can afford premium subscription and smartphone)
- **Education:** Varied, but tech-comfortable
- **Device:** Smartphone users (iOS and Android)

### Psychographics
- **Values:** Health, self-improvement, autonomy, authenticity
- **Interests:** Fitness, wellness, personal development, technology
- **Lifestyle:** Busy professionals, students, parents wanting to set better examples
- **Personality:** Self-aware, motivated but struggling, open to guidance
- **Mindset:** Have tried quitting before and failed; know they need help but sceptical of traditional methods

## User Personas

### Persona 1: Alex the Young Professional
- **Age:** 24
- **Occupation:** Marketing coordinator
- **Goals:** Quit vaping to improve fitness, save money, and stop feeling dependent on nicotine
- **Pain Points:** Started vaping socially in university, now addicted. Embarrassed about the habit. Generic apps feel impersonal and easy to ignore. Can't afford ongoing therapy.
- **How We Help:** Personalised coach that fits into their busy schedule, understands their specific triggers (work stress, social situations), and provides real accountability without judgment.

### Persona 2: Sam the Concerned Parent
- **Age:** 38
- **Occupation:** Project manager
- **Goals:** Quit smoking/vaping to be healthier for their kids and stop hiding the habit
- **Pain Points:** Has tried patches, gum, and cold turkey multiple times. Allen Carr felt dated. Feels shame about repeated failures. Needs something that adapts when life gets stressful.
- **How We Help:** A non-judgmental coach that learns from setbacks, celebrates small wins, and provides science-backed motivation. Premium service signals commitment and increases follow-through.

### Persona 3: Jordan the Health-Conscious Quitter
- **Age:** 29
- **Occupation:** Fitness instructor
- **Goals:** Eliminate the cognitive dissonance of being "healthy" while vaping
- **Pain Points:** Knows all the health facts but struggles with the psychological addiction. Needs mental frameworks, not just information. Finds medical interventions too invasive.
- **How We Help:** Positive psychology techniques and personalised mental strategies. Progress tracking that connects to health improvements they care about.

## Customer Journey

### Awareness
- Social media content (TikTok, Instagram) with edgy, relatable messaging
- Word of mouth from successful quitters
- Search for "how to quit vaping" or "quit nicotine app"
- Influencer partnerships in health/wellness space
- Reddit communities (r/QuitVaping, r/stopsmoking)

### Consideration
- Brand resonates emotionally ("I Fucking Quit" captures their frustration and determination)
- Reviews and success stories from real users
- Free trial or preview of personalised coach experience
- Comparison against free apps (more personalised) and human coaches (more affordable)

### Decision
- ROI calculation: subscription cost vs. money spent on nicotine
- Premium price signals quality and commitment
- Low-friction signup and engaging onboarding
- Immediate connection with their personalised coach

### Retention
- Daily check-ins create habit and accountability
- Visible progress (health improvements, money saved, streak tracking)
- Gamification elements (milestones, awards)
- Coach adapts and learns from their journey
- Community or shared success stories
- The relationship with their coach becomes meaningful

## Value Proposition

### Problems We Solve
- Quitting nicotine is hard: only 3-6% succeed without support
- Generic apps are easy to ignore and don't adapt to individual needs
- Human specialists are too expensive for ongoing support
- Self-help books feel outdated or preachy
- Medical interventions feel invasive
- Users have failed before and lost confidence

### Our Solution
- AI-powered personal cessation coach available 24/7
- Hyper-personalised support that learns and adapts
- Proactive check-ins that create real accountability
- Grounded in positive psychology and behavioural science
- Progress tracking with meaningful health and financial metrics
- Engaging chat experience with rich media support

### Why Us
- **Personalisation at scale:** Unlike generic apps, IFQ learns your triggers, patterns, and what motivates you
- **Authentic brand voice:** "I Fucking Quit" speaks to the frustration and determination of our users—no corporate wellness speak
- **Proactive, not reactive:** Your coach reaches out to you, creating accountability that passive apps can't match
- **Science-backed approach:** Combines the best of positive psychology with real-time adaptation
- **Premium positioning:** Higher price point attracts committed users and signals quality—but still far cheaper than human coaching or ongoing nicotine costs
