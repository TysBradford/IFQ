# Project Overview

IFQ - I Fucking Quit - is a virtual, personalised cessation coach to help people quite their nicotine addiction.


## Vision

IFQ will be THE recognised service for cessation.
This will be a combination of a product that truly delivers to it's users AND a unbeatable brand and distribution strategy.

## Problem Being Solved

Quitting nicotine is incredibly difficult. 
Many people with an addiction have tried and failed to quit previously.

IFQ solves this through:
- Proactive and constant hyper-personalised support
- Accountability
- Best practices in positive psychology
- Providing meaningful scientific information to inform and empower users

Alternative cessation methods fail because:
- Advice and support personalised enough to the individual (standard tracking apps)
- Outdated or misaligned tone of deliver (Allen Carrs books)
- Too expensive (human specialist)
- Invasive (medical treatments)


**The Scale of the Problem:**
- 1.2 billion people use tobacco globally, with 100+ million now vaping
- Less than 10% of quit attempts succeed each year
- Only 3-6% succeed when quitting without support
- People typically make 6-30 attempts before successfully quitting

## Who's It For?

People who have a nicotine addiction and are motivated to quit.
This will mostly be people using vapes - a recent pandemic in nicotine usage.
Due to App Store requirements, people need to be 18+ to use this service.

Reasons they want to give up nicotine include:
- Physical health concerns
- Mental and cognitive improvements
- Cost savings
- Freedom from being controlled by a substance

## How It Works

IFQ is a mobile app.
Through an in-depth onboarding, your virtual cessation coach (who will be named and have an avatar, personality etc.) will then guide you through the quitting process.
Your coach will check in multiple times a day to get progress updates and provide encouragement and support.
Via these interactions, the coach will learn and course correct accordingly to ensure your stay on the right path.

The app will also surface progress and have some mild gamification tactics to encourage progress.
You will get notifications and awards for milestones, coupled with health and financial progress.

There will be a combination of a chat interface and info/tracker screens in the app.
The chat should support rich content like images/gifs/videos.
The chat will connect to the AI agent which will respond in real time, and also push out messages unprompted at certain times.

## Cost

The app will work on a subscription service. Subscriptions will be monthly, or a reduced annual rate.
The cost will be ROI positive versus the costs of nicotine.
It will be on the higher end on the spectrum versus other digital alternatives.
